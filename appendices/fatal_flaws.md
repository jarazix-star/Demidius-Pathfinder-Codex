# Fatal Flaws

## Save DC

Demigod Fatal Flaw DC is 20 + caster level. The Eyebrow Piercing of Confidence adds 5, so at caster level 17 the DC is 42.

## FF-01 — Daily Gambling

Demidius must gamble each day. Satisfy this under controlled conditions before critical missions.

## FF-02 — Battle Commitment

After entering a fight, Demidius must save to voluntarily stop. Establish party extraction authority and prearranged disengagement procedures.

## FF-03 — Misjudging Intentions

Demidius takes -20 when judging the intentions of women or effeminate men. Important conclusions require independent verification.

## FF-04 — Paranoia

Source: Key of Daedalus. On a failed save Demidius must act on paranoia, such as spying or taking excessive precautions.

## FF-05 — Loyalty

Source: sleeping in the inherited demiplane. Triggered only in relation to the owner, Demidius. Confirmed for Demidius and Aristea.
