# Notable Figures

## Queen Lidda Beaumont of Nysia

Queen of Nysia, Slayer of War, and Minor Goddess of Dueling (War Subdomain). She slew Ares at the opening of the Culling and later gifted Demidius the Glasses of Beaumont.

## Ares

God of War slain by Queen Beaumont during the Culling outside Tradegulf.

## Hermes

Demidius's patron deity and source of Seven-Pipped Gem, the custom divine progression, and Hermes's Boots of Speed.
