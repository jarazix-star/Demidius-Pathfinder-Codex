# Campaign Prophecies

Prophecies record visions and warnings, not automatically inevitable future events. Each entry distinguishes the witnessed vision, its interpretation, the party's response, and what has actually occurred.

## Maarin's first Dark Prophecy: the death of Filius

Maarin rolled **Dark Prophecy** as a Fatal Flaw and received her first vision shortly after returning from Yuletown.

### Vision sequence

1. Maarin floated above the turning world. Time accelerated and ten years passed in an instant.
2. A stone-covered man carrying a staff that appeared to be a branch of Yggdrasil stood before a cloaked figure armed with a golden scythe.
3. The cloaked figure struck the stone-covered man down. As he fell and his stone armor crumbled, the world shook and split open with a primal scream.
4. Gaia awakened, towered over the world, and wept and screamed over the body. Her stomp opened a rift from which gargantuan giants emerged with humanoid torsos and serpentine legs or lower bodies.
5. Across years of accelerated visions, Gaia, the giants, and the Primordials hunted and destroyed every pantheon.
6. The cosmos protested the destruction and the Material Plane began to shatter.
7. A shadowed figure hovering outside the Material Plane watched with eyes like swirling lights and galaxies. The figure said it was time to write of the experiment and hoped the next would go better.
8. The figure snapped its fingers, erased the Material Plane and all existence, and initiated a new cosmos with a loud bang.

### Identification and response

The vision led the party to **Filius**. The stone-covered man is therefore identified with Filius, an Oread mortal god of the World Tree. The staff's appearance connects him to Yggdrasil, but the current record does not confirm that his actual staff is literally one of its branches.

Filius is presently alive and serving as a healer aboard the *Matcha Frappuccino*. Finding him may have created an opportunity to avert the vision, but the record does not establish that the predicted chain has been permanently prevented.

### Unresolved elements

- Whether the ten-year interval is literal, symbolic, conditional, or measured from the vision.
- The identity, allegiance, and motive of the cloaked golden-scythe wielder.
- Why Filius's death would awaken Gaia or unleash the anguiped giants.
- Whether the giants are a named people, Gaia's children, primordial servants, or another force.
- Which Primordials join Gaia and whether any oppose her.
- Why destroying the pantheons destabilizes the Material Plane and cosmos.
- The identity and nature of the galaxy-eyed observer outside reality.
- What the observer means by an “experiment,” who conducts it, and whether earlier or parallel creations exist.
- Whether the final erasure is fate, a conditional failure state, or one possible future.

## Okeanikos prophecy

Maarin later received a prophecy of Okeanikos's coming birth. Whoever recovered his egg would have great influence over what he became. The party rescued him, and Maarin took him as her cohort. Its exact wording and relationship to the Dark Prophecy Fatal Flaw remain unrecorded.
