# Campaign Timeline

## CY-2026-06-28 — The Culling: Beaumont Slays Ares

Outside the port of Tradegulf, Ares challenged Queen Lidda Beaumont of Nysia. Beaumont offered him a final opportunity to withdraw and seek reconciliation with the New Gods. Ares refused and attacked. Beaumont drew a longsword with red tassels, referred to it as "our blade" while addressing Perlot, and decapitated Ares in one blow.

The heavens proclaimed Lidda Beaumont **Queen of Nysia, Slayer of War, and Minor Goddess of Dueling (War Subdomain)**.

### Consequences

- Ares was slain during the Culling.
- Beaumont ascended as a minor goddess.
- Her alliance with Demidius later included the gift of the Glasses of Beaumont.

## Other campaign milestones tracked elsewhere

- Demidius inherited his deceased brother's demiplane.
- The Key of Daedalus was acquired and used, permanently granting Paranoia.
- Aristea died and her soul became trapped in Tartarus.
