# Build Philosophy

Demidius is a Charisma-centered Oracle, mythic controller, party face, captain, and demigod. His comparative advantage is certainty and leverage rather than personal damage.

## Priority order

1. Remove enemy magical defenses.
2. Preserve action economy and counter enemy recovery.
3. Make pivotal rolls succeed through probability control.
4. Enable the party's stronger damage dealers and specialists.
5. Use enchantment, battlefield control, or diplomacy after resistance is stripped.
6. Expand campaign-scale influence through leadership, artifacts, and infrastructure.

## Current retraining direction

- Retrain Deceitful.
- Retrain Conceal Spell unless a sustained intrigue arc makes it essential.
- Retrain Diverse Obedience if the custom divine system fully replaces its unique value.
- Strongly consider retraining Divine Fighting Technique because the rapier is a backup weapon.

## Rule dependencies

This doctrine depends on CR-01 through CR-15, especially the Probability Engine rulings, wizard-as-divine access, Steadfast Personality, and Demidius's role definition.
