# Campaign Assumptions

- Pathfinder 1e with mythic rules and extensive compatible third-party material.
- Progression continues beyond level 20.
- Demidius can cast wizard spells as divine spells (CR-09).
- The campaign uses a custom Hermes obedience/divine-power system (CR-11).
- Epic Leadership is active (CR-12).
- Fortune's Child and Fate's Favored both increase all qualifying luck bonuses at this table (CR-01–CR-04).
- Steadfast Personality adds full Charisma modifier to all Will saves (CR-15).
- Demigod Fatal Flaws use the campaign DC formula in CR-16.
- Campaign artifacts and strategic assets are authoritative as recorded in the artifact and infrastructure systems.
- The campaign takes place on Zatera in 150 P.A.; P.A. means Post-Arrival.
- The Arrival may be Fel's coming from the Outer Realms or Cronus's release; do not equate year 0 with the divine schism until confirmed.
- Poseidon's Shipbreaker Sea curse disables ordinary compasses and obscures the stars and moon each night.
- Fourteen Wayfinders function within the curse: one for each of the seven Pirate Kings or Queens and one for each second-in-command at campaign start.
- Sounon lies within the Sunlit Chain and is the intended shared home of the Dawnrunner and *Matcha Frappuccino* crews.
- Declan is dead, the heroes hold his Wayfinder, and the Sunlit Chain succession remains unresolved.
