# Campaign Assumptions

- Pathfinder 1e with mythic rules and extensive compatible third-party material.
- Progression continues beyond level 20.
- Demidius can cast wizard spells as divine spells (CR-09).
- The campaign uses a custom Hermes obedience/divine-power system (CR-11).
- Epic Leadership is active (CR-12).
- Fortune's Child and Fate's Favored both increase all qualifying luck bonuses at this table (CR-01–CR-04).
- Steadfast Personality adds full Charisma modifier to all Will saves (CR-15).
- Demigod Fatal Flaws use the campaign DC formula in CR-16.
- Campaign artifacts and strategic assets are authoritative as recorded in the artifact and infrastructure systems.
