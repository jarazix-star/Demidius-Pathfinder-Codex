# Consumables and Limited Resources

Consumables require explicit quantity tracking because using them permanently changes Demidius's strategic inventory.

| Consumable | Held by Demidius | Role | Detailed entry |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Blood of the Lernaean Hydra | 2 doses | Extreme single-target poison; opening save versus death followed by Constitution damage | [Entry](../systems/consumables/BLOOD_OF_THE_LERNAEAN_HYDRA.md) |

## Recording standard

For each limited resource, keep its physical quantity, mechanical doses, custody, delivery method, confirmed rules, unresolved interpretations, and expenditure history distinct. Never infer that one container equals one dose unless the campaign record confirms it.
