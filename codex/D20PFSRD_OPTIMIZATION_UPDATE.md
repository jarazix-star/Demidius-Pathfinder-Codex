# d20PFSRD Optimization Update

## Decision summary

The d20PFSRD corpus is a discovery index rather than an authority tier. Three findings directly affect Demidius's current planning, but each occupies a different role.

| Option | Rating | Role | Current decision |
|---|---:|---|---|
| *Wandering weather* | S | Mobile weather and fleet logistics | Acquire as soon as possible. The mobile-center clause is verified; numerical ship speed remains a campaign ruling. |
| *Billow the sail* | S, conditional | Direct galleon propulsion | Ask for third-party approval. If approved, use its explicit 25% speed increase rather than inventing a conversion from general wind rules. |
| *Calculated luck* | A, conditional | Repeated caster-level checks | Prepare for planned sequences of dispels or spell-resistance checks when pre-casting is possible and the random vulnerability is acceptable. |

## Engine integration

### Probability

*Calculated luck* can produce a +2 luck bonus on caster-level checks for 1 round per level. Under CR-01 and CR-02, that qualifying bonus should become +4. At least one desired 6 appears on 3d8 about 33% of the time.

This does not replace Seven-Pipped Gem. The Gem supplies a much larger post-roll intervention for one decisive check. *Calculated luck* is valuable when Demidius expects several caster-level checks during its duration and can cast before initiative or otherwise afford the setup action.

### Magical supremacy

A successful *calculated luck* setup can support multiple dispel checks, counterspell-related caster-level checks, and attempts to overcome spell resistance. It combines particularly well with effects that allow rolling twice, but ordinary luck-bonus stacking rules still apply.

### Infrastructure and mobility

*Wandering weather* is the official Paizo choice for carrying controlled weather with a moving caster and therefore with the Dawnrunner. Its text does not specify a percentage increase to vessel speed.

Frog God Games' *billow the sail* is the cleanest direct propulsion mechanic found: one ship, 1 hour per caster level, continual favorable wind, and an explicit 25% sailing-speed increase. It requires GM approval, a ruling on the Dawnrunner workbook's multiplication order, and a ruling on interaction with other favorable-wind effects.

## Priority order

1. Acquire *wandering weather* immediately for its broad strategic role.
2. Ask the GM whether *billow the sail* is approved and how its 25% increase enters the ship formula.
3. Add *calculated luck* to the prepared-access review for planned magical-supremacy encounters.
4. Do not spend resources on the d20PFSRD “Storm Pilot” trait as a speed solution; it is Paizo's renamed *Abendego Pilot* and does not directly increase ship speed.
