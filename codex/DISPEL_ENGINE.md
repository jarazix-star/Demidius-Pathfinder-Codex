# Dispel and Counterspell Engine

## Primary package

1. **Mythic Greater Dispel Magic** - Spell Perfection target and default anti-magic action.
2. **Eldritch Breach** - roll twice on dispel and related caster-level checks.
3. **Dispel Synergy / Dispel Synergy (Mythic)** - turns successful stripping into party-wide save pressure.
4. **Destructive Dispel / mythic upgrade** - adds a disabling rider after reliability is secured.
5. **Flexible Counterspell** or the mythic spell's immediate-action augment - prevent enemy recovery and escape.
6. **Bound by Honor** - possible mythic-power fuel for rerolls, augments, and counters.
7. **Inexplicable Luck (Mythic)** - emergency reliability on the decisive check.
8. **Seven-Pipped Gem** - a +10 luck burst on any d20 at 17 HD; may be declared after the die but before the result (CR-05).
9. **Luckstone** - permanent +3 on covered checks under CR-06.
10. **Eyebrow Piercing of Confidence** - +6 Charisma improves uses, DCs, and supporting statistics (CR-07).

## Spell Perfection ruling

Spell Perfection improves the selected spell according to its own wording. It does not automatically double traits, item bonuses, obedience boons, mythic path abilities, or class features.

## Boss sequence

Identify -> Strip -> Dispel Synergy -> Ally save effect -> Reserve immediate counter -> Lock battlefield.


## Campaign-rule interactions

- **CR-04:** A qualifying competence, insight, or morale bonus converted by Make Your Own Luck gains +2 from Fortune's Child and Fate's Favored.
- **CR-05:** Seven-Pipped Gem can add +10 luck to the decisive dispel or caster-level check at 17 HD.
- **CR-07:** The legendary item's +6 Charisma increases the number of Gem uses and strengthens the oracle chassis.
- **CR-09:** Wizard anti-magic and control spells are valid divine recommendations.
- **CR-10:** Spell Perfection does not double unrelated luck, item, trait, or obedience bonuses.

## Immediate-action conflict

Seven-Pipped Gem, Flexible Counterspell, and the immediate-action augment of Mythic Greater Dispel Magic compete for the same immediate action. At the start of each enemy turn, decide whether the higher-value response is protecting Demidius's next roll or interrupting the enemy spell.
