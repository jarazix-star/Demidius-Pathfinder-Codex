# Enchantment and Mental Defense Engine

## Standard plan

1. Detect or infer immunity/protection.
2. Remove dispellable protection such as mind blank.
3. Apply Dispel Synergy or another saving-throw penalty.
4. Use the appropriate enchantment or control spell.

## Immunity precedents

Monster abilities such as Bypass Mental Defenses are useful precedents for a custom Hermes/demigod boon, but are not automatically player options.

## Relevant verified options

- Role Reversal: reverse failed charm/compulsion effects.
- False Allegiance: resist influence and deceive the enemy into believing it succeeded.
- Ability Focus (Mythic): strong precedent for save-DC enhancement and forcing two saves on a special ability.
- Piercing Spell (Mythic): helps against spell resistance, though not immunity.
