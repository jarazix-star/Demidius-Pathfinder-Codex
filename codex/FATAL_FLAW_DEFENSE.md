# Fatal Flaw Defense

## Save engine

- Base DC: `20 + caster level`.
- Eyebrow Piercing modifier: +5 DC.
- Current DC at CL 17: **42**.
- Save: Will.
- Steadfast Personality adds the full Charisma modifier to all Will saves.
- The Seven-Pipped Gem may apply because the table permits it on any d20 roll, subject to immediate-action availability.

## Flaw matrix

| Flaw | Trigger/effect | Strategic risk | Primary mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Daily gambling | Must gamble each day | Low if scheduled | Resolve a controlled wager early each day |
| Battle commitment | Must save to stop once engaged in a fight | High | Preauthorized extraction plan; contingency and ally intervention |
| Misjudging intentions | -20 judging women or effeminate men's intentions | Very high in diplomacy | Independent Sense Motive, divination, written review protocol |
| Paranoia | On failed save, spy or take excessive precautions | High at campaign scale | Trusted-officer review, decision deadlines, objective evidence standards |

## Party procedures

1. Major agreements involving a person covered by the -20 flaw require review by a trusted ally.
2. The captain's battle-extraction plan should be agreed before combat begins.
3. A failed Paranoia save should trigger a second-signature rule for major strategic decisions.
4. Daily gambling should be satisfied deliberately rather than left to an uncontrolled opportunity.
