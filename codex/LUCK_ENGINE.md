# Luck Engine

## Core elements

- Fate's Favored: +1 to qualifying luck bonuses.
- Divine favor and other explicit luck bonuses.
- Fortune's Child and the Seven-Pipped Gem route.
- Make Your Own Luck: convert competence, insight, or morale bonuses into luck bonuses.
- Inexplicable Luck (Mythic): emergency d20 certainty.
- Bound by Honor: additional mythic power can fuel luck and reroll mechanics.

## Stacking protocol

Record the original bonus type, converted type, source, duration, and whether another luck bonus is already active. Luck bonuses generally do not stack with each other unless an effect explicitly says otherwise.

## GM-review issues

- Whether Fate's Favored applies after Make Your Own Luck converts the bonus.
- Exact Seven-Pipped Gem and Fortune's Child wording.
