# Mythic Spell Selection Engine

## Selection rule

Demidius should select spells based on **problem solved per action**, not damage per slot.

### Priority order

1. Anti-magic and counterspelling.
2. Immediate/swift/contingent action economy.
3. No-save or durable battlefield control.
4. Enchantment after immunity-granting effects are stripped.
5. Long-duration party buffs and defensive infrastructure.
6. Planar travel, divination, rescue, and headquarters support.
7. Direct damage only when it carries decisive control or bypass properties.

## Core packages

### Anti-magic opener

- *Mythic greater dispel magic* - S+.
- *Mage's disjunction* - A+, with collateral-risk warning.
- *Break enchantment* - A for hostile effects and conditions.
- *Antimagic field* - B+ because it suppresses Demidius too.

### Lockdown follow-up

- *Forcecage* - S.
- *Maze* - S.
- *Wall of force* - A+.
- *Dimensional lock* - A+.
- *Prismatic wall/sphere* - A+.
- *Binding* and *imprisonment* - A for permanent containment.

### Enchantment finisher

- *Euphoric tranquility* - S.
- *Overwhelming presence* - S.
- *Dominate monster* - A+.
- *Mass charm monster* - A.

These move up only after mind blank, immunity-granting buffs, and spell turning are removed.

### Luck and certainty

- *Borrow fortune* - A+.
- *Beacon of luck* - A+.
- *Embrace destiny* - A.
- *Moment of prescience* - A+.

Always record the bonus type and Fate's Favored interaction before stacking.

### Infrastructure and rescue

- *Permanency* - S.
- *Forbiddance* - A+.
- *Gate* - A+.
- *Plane shift* - A.
- *Discern location* - A+.
- *Freedom* - A+.
- *True resurrection* - A+ after the soul is released.
