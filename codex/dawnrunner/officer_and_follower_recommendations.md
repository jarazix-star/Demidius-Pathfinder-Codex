# Dawnrunner Officer and Follower Recommendations

## Tier 1: Recruit or Retrain Soon

### Navigator Ranger
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 7.  
**Role:** Master Navigator or Sailing Master.  
**Reason:** The archetype directly improves voyage efficiency, encounter avoidance, crew initiative, awareness, and ship-related rerolls.

### Ship’s Officer Cavalier
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 2.  
**Role:** Marine captain, flag officer, or second-line command.  
**Reason:** Converts pennant and inspire-courage mechanics into ship-wide force multiplication.

### Crown Privateer Paladin
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF pp. 6–7.  
**Role:** Thorne Knight commander or sacred defender.
**Reason:** Extends paladin auras across the entire ship and grants shared sacred defenses.

## Tier 2: Strong Specialists

### Lookout Rogue
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 8.  
**Role:** Crow’s-nest lookout and illusion spotter.

### Order of the Griffon Cavalier
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Cavaliers’ Orders*, PDF pp. 4–5.  
**Role:** Freedom champion, anti-slavery knight, aerial response officer.

### Superior Tactician
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Cavaliers’ Orders*, PDF p. 9.  
**Role:** Feat for any teamwork-focused cavalier officer.

### Eidolon Transport Specialist
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Extra Evolutions*, PDF pp. 2–6.  
**Role:** Summoner follower using Howdah, Hidden Compartment, Guise, Mimicry, and Camouflage for transport, infiltration, and concealed logistics.

## Tier 3: Campaign Approval Required

### Spagyric Quartermaster
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Alchemists’ Discoveries*, PDF pp. 4–6.  
**Role:** Mission-specific device outfitting for officers and specialists.  
**Caution:** This can substantially increase organizational equipment flexibility and requires a clear GM ruling.
