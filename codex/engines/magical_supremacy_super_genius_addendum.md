# Magical Supremacy Engine — Super Genius Addendum

## New Highest-Value Tools

### Doom Gaze
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Witches’ Hexes*, PDF p. 4.  
Potentially imposes a scaling penalty to saving throws, reaching –8 at 16th witch level. This is the strongest newly found setup effect for Demidius’s enchantments, provided a legal acquisition route exists.

### Mock
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Witches’ Hexes*, PDF p. 5.  
Staggering a target attacks its action economy rather than its statistics. Track the mind-affecting and language-dependent descriptors.

### Dark Desires
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Patron Hexes*, PDF p. 2.  
Forces concentration checks across spellcasting, spell-like abilities, and many mentally demanding actions. Strong against casters, skill specialists, and enemies dependent on complex activated abilities.

### Rune Hex
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Witches’ Hexes*, PDF p. 5.  
Allows a known hex to become a prepared trigger, expanding Magical Supremacy into pre-combat security and battlefield shaping.

## Recommendation Change

No existing dispel recommendation is displaced. The new options form a complementary sequence:

1. Apply a save or action-economy debuff.
2. Remove defensive magic with Mythic Greater Dispel Magic.
3. Land the encounter-ending enchantment or control effect.
4. Use Rune Hex and infrastructure to prepare for recurring threats.
