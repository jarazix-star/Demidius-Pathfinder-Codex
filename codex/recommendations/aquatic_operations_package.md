# Aquatic Operations Package

**Rating:** A for submerged missions

**Target:** Dawnrunner and Matcha Frappuccino crews

**Source:** *Aquatic Adventures*, PDF pp. 48-49, 52, and 62-63 / printed pp. 46-47, 50, and 60-61.

**Verification:** Underwater spell-adjudication page visually verified; archetype, spell, pressure, and equipment entries reviewed in context.

Before a submerged mission, confirm the table's rules for breath and verbal components, weather and cloud magic, cold, electricity, fire-to-steam conversion, invisible creatures displacing water, and underwater Perception. Prepare invisibility bubble for covert movement and pressure adaptation or stabilize pressure for emergency depth changes.

Aquachymist is useful research for underwater bombs, but Binky cannot add it to gun chemist without a full archetype compatibility audit.
