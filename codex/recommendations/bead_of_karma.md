# Bead of Karma

**Rating:** S

**Target:** Demidius

**Source:** *Core Rulebook* (PZO1110), PDF pp. 531-532 / printed pp. 530-531.
**Verification:** Rule text reviewed in context.

A divine caster can activate the bead to gain +4 caster level for 10 minutes. It is excellent preparation for a scheduled dispel, counterspell, spell-resistance, or duration-sensitive sequence. It does not grant higher spell levels, slots, or spells known.
