# Billow the Sail

**Rating:** S, conditional on third-party approval

**Priority:** Ask the GM to approve and add it to Demidius's ship-support package

**Verification:** Verified against the local *Book of Lost Spells* PDF; d20PFSRD independently confirms the spell entry and Section 15 attribution
**Source:** *Book of Lost Spells* (Frog God Games), PDF p. 26 / printed p. 25; [d20PFSRD spell entry](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/3rd-party-spells/frog-god-games/billow-the-sail/)

## Why it matters

*Billow the sail* is a 3rd-level sorcerer/wizard spell that targets one ship for 1 hour per caster level. It creates a continual, localized favorable wind in the caster's chosen direction and explicitly increases a sailing ship's speed by 25%.

That is a cleaner propulsion rule than trying to infer a numerical benefit from *control weather*. At Demidius's current level, one casting covers most of a day, and CR-09 supplies access to wizard spells as divine spells. The explicit percentage can be applied to the Dawnrunner workbook once the GM approves the third-party source and confirms the multiplication order.

## Limits and interactions

- This is Frog God Games material, not Paizo material; source approval remains mandatory.
- The spell supplies favorable wind rather than an untyped speed bonus. Do not assume it stacks with another favorable-wind effect such as *wandering weather*.
- Confirm whether the 25% applies before or after the campaign's Magic Swiftness upgrade and Profession (sailor) modifier.
- The spell targets one ship. Separate castings are required for the Dawnrunner and Matcha Frappuccino.

## Disposition

For pure cruising speed, *billow the sail* is the strongest directly worded discovery in this web pass. Keep *wandering weather* for its much larger weather-control role; use *billow the sail* as the preferred propulsion spell if the GM approves it.
