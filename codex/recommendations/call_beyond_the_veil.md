# Call Beyond the Veil

**Rating:** A for the Aristea operation

**Target:** Planar recovery team

**Source:** *Occult Adventures* (PZO1132), PDF pp. 211-212 / printed pp. 210-211.
**Verification:** Complete ritual entry reviewed in context.

This sixth-level occult ritual can call a deceased spirit or psychic entity from an extraplanar location into the primary caster to answer questions. It requires difficult Diplomacy, Knowledge (religion), and Knowledge (planes) checks, a personal object or true name, up to eight secondary casters, and carries exhaustion and negative-level risks.

It may establish communication with Aristea in Tartarus, but it cannot free her soul. The spirit can resist, refuse, or lie, and Eris or Tartarus may block the attempt. Treat it as intelligence gathering before a rescue, with containment and recovery measures ready.
