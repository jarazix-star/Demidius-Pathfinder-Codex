# Counter-Divination Rooms

**Rating:** S for a Sounon war room

**Target:** Sounon, Key of Daedalus staging, and fleet command

**Source:** *Inner Sea Intrigue*, PDF p. 59 / printed p. 57.

**Verification:** Complete construction entries reviewed in context and visually verified.

Secrecy runes block sound and divinations up to 3rd, 5th, or 7th level even while portals are open. This makes a superior rune-inscribed room the leading published model for campaign planning and planar staging. Lead-lined rooms block divinations only when seamlessly closed and become airtight, but are a cheaper vault option.

Neither construction should be assumed to block artifacts, deities, mythic exceptions, or spells above its stated protection. The final design needs ventilation, physical security, dispel detection, and an emergency exit.
