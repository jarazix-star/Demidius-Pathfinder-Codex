# Dark Desires

**Rating:** A+  
**Verification:** Verified source; patron/acquisition unresolved  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Patron Hexes*, PDF p. 2  
**Primary role:** Anti-caster and concentration denial

## Analysis

Forces concentration checks when the target casts, uses spell-like abilities, uses many mental or Dexterity skills, or performs other concentration-heavy actions. The DC scales as 10 + Intelligence modifier + witch level. A Will save negates and patron restrictions apply, but the breadth of disrupted actions makes this an excellent control-boon candidate.
