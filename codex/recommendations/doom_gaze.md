# Doom Gaze

**Rating:** S-  
**Verification:** Verified source; acquisition unresolved  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Witches’ Hexes*, PDF p. 4  
**Primary role:** Demidius save-debuff setup

## Analysis

At 16th witch level the chosen penalty reaches –8, including an option to penalize saving throws. If Demidius can acquire it through Spirit Guide, a legal hex-expansion route, or a custom divine boon, it becomes one of the strongest opening effects for enchantment and control. It is once per target per day and a successful Will save reduces duration rather than necessarily negating all value.
