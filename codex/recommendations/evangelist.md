# Evangelist

**Rating:** S conditional  
**Priority:** First post-20 divine prestige-class review  
**Verification:** Printed rules reviewed in context; campaign access unresolved  
**Source:** *Inner Sea Gods* (PZO9267), PDF pp. 199–200 / printed pp. 198–199  
**Primary role:** Advance Hermes devotion while preserving Demidius's base-class engine

## Analysis

Evangelist advances the features of one aligned base class at every prestige-class level after the first while retaining its own Hit Die, base attack bonus, saves, and skills. It also accelerates the three deity boons and culminates in Spiritual Form.

This makes it the most promising published prestige-class lead for Demidius if the campaign allows post-20 prestige advancement. It still costs one level of aligned-class advancement, and it requires Deific Obedience plus an approved Hermes obedience and boon package.

## Campaign boundary

Deific Obedience boons are not the campaign's spendable Boons. Evangelist does not make Demidius Hermes's unique Champion and does not replace the two trials that made him Blessed.

## Disposition

Ask the GM whether epic progression can enter Evangelist and whether a custom Hermes obedience may use the lesser-deity adaptation guidance in *Inner Sea Gods*. Do not select it until its interaction with oracle progression, mythic advancement, and campaign divine powers is explicit.
