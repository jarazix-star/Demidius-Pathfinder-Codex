# Four-Leaf Clover

**Rating:** A+

**Target:** Demidius

**Source:** *Occult Adventures* (PZO1132), PDF p. 259 / printed p. 258.
**Verification:** Rule text reviewed in context.

This slotless 3,750 gp item provides a +2 luck bonus on an ability check, saving throw, or skill check three times per day as a free action, even outside the user's turn. Under CR-01 and CR-02, Demidius's bonus is expected to become +4.

Luck bonuses do not normally stack, so it replaces rather than adds to his persistent +3 luckstone bonus. Its real gains are the marginal +1 and preserving the immediate action for another defense.
