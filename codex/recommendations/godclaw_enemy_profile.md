# Godclaw Enemy Profile

**Rating:** S intelligence priority

**Target:** Demidius's Hellknight defenses

**Source:** *Path of the Hellknight*, PDF pp. 28-29 and 41 / printed pp. 26-27 and 39.

**Verification:** Godclaw mystery page visually verified; Scrutinize Spell reviewed in context.

Published Godclaw oracles can combine obedience benefits drawn from several members of the Godclaw and may possess instant heavy armor, coercive suggestion effects, saving-throw rerolls, and defensive revelations. This supports preparing for heavily armored divine casters with layered portfolios rather than a single predictable patron.

This is an enemy capability profile, not proof that every pursuer has oracle levels. Scrutinize Spell also shows that trained Hellknight casters may identify opposing spell lists with high reliability.
