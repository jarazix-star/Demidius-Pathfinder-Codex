# Iroran Paladin Anti-Unluck Auras

**Rating:** A allied build  
**Priority:** Consider for a follower, cohort, or allied paladin  
**Verification:** Printed archetype abilities reviewed in context  
**Source:** *Inner Sea Combat* (PZO9268), PDF p. 41 / printed p. 39  
**Primary role:** Defense against forced rerolls and worse-of-two-roll effects

## Analysis

Aura of Excellence protects its paladin from effects that force rerolls or demand the worse of two results and helps nearby allies resist those effects. Aura of Perfection later improves protection from forced second-result effects. This is a rare, direct defensive answer to the campaign's luck and unluck threats.

The archetype is not a practical Demidius selection. Its value is as a build direction for an allied paladin or a source-balanced model for a custom protective aura.

## Disposition

Keep on the allied-recruitment shortlist. Confirm alignment, deity, archetype compatibility, and exact aura interaction before building a specific NPC around it.
