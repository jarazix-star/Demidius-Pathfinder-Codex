# Masked Persona Operations

**Rating:** A+

**Target:** Alley, Roy, and covert crew teams

**Source:** *Inner Sea Intrigue*, PDF pp. 14-15 / printed pp. 12-13.

**Verification:** Complete general subsystem and associated feat page reviewed and visually verified.

Any class can establish a consistent alternate identity. Once established through a level of activity, it can separate reputation and several divinations from the character's ordinary identity. Convincing Persona protects against ordinary compromise and improves Bluff and Disguise; Masked Symbol supplies a concept-based benefit.

Alley should direct identity compartmentalization, while Roy is a natural social infiltrator. Keep knowledge of each identity tightly limited and do not assume the subsystem is active until the GM approves it.
