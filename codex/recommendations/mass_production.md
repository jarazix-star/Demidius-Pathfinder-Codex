# Mass Production

**Rating:** S-  
**Verification:** Verified source; requires Spagyric Devices  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Alchemists’ Discoveries*, PDF p. 6  
**Primary role:** Follower equipment multiplication

## Analysis

The first device of a type counts at full cost, the second and third at half, and later copies at quarter cost against the device limit. This changes Spagyric Devices from personal adaptability into an organizational outfitting engine.
