# Navigator

**Rating:** S  
**Verification:** Verified source; follower recommendation  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 7

## Analysis

Reduces voyage time or random-encounter chance by up to 30%, gives the bonded crew +2 initiative and key awareness skills, and grants rerolls on ship-related attacks, checks, and saves. This is the strongest direct organizational addition found in the first two batches.
