# Otherworldly Kimono

**Rating:** S

**Target:** Demidius

**Source:** *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF pp. 217-218 / printed pp. 216-217.
**Verification:** Rule text reviewed in context and the relevant page visually checked.

The kimono provides a +4 untyped bonus on all caster-level checks and +4 resistance on saves. Its daily extradimensional imprisonment effect has no initial save; while the target remains trapped, both bonuses become +6.

This directly improves dispels and checks to overcome spell resistance. Resolve its body-slot conflict, cost, and the risk of the captive escaping before treating the +6 state as reliable.
