# Profession (Sailor) Skill Unlock

**Rating:** S conditional

**Target:** Demidius and the Dawnrunner

**Source:** *Pathfinder Unchained* (PZO1131), PDF pp. 83 and 87 / printed pp. 82 and 86.
**Verification:** Skill-unlock and Profession entries reviewed in context.

With Signature Skill and 10 ranks, the Profession unlock permits two rolls and the better result on Profession checks and allows taking 10 on questions about the profession. If the campaign adopts skill unlocks and the Dawnrunner's operational checks are Profession (sailor) checks, this is a major reliability upgrade.

Do not add it to the character sheet until both conditions are confirmed. The separate background-skills subsystem could refund ordinary ranks invested in Profession, but is also a global optional rule.
