# Riftwarden Specialist

**Rating:** A organizational fit  
**Priority:** Recruit or train before sustained planar operations  
**Verification:** Printed prestige-class role and prerequisites reviewed in context  
**Source:** *Paths of Prestige* (PZO9249), PDF pp. 48–49  
**Primary role:** Secure portals, planar corridors, and staging sites

## Analysis

Riftwardens specialize in abjuration, planar knowledge, and resisting evil outsiders. Their training is well suited to guarding the Key of Daedalus's routes, hardening a Sounon gateway, and supporting any expedition into Tartarus.

The class's prerequisites and lost opportunity make it a weak immediate rebuild for Demidius. Its highest value is organizational: recruit a qualified caster, sponsor a follower's training, or establish a small Riftwarden-inspired portal watch.

## Disposition

Add planar-security expertise to future recruitment. Before opening a persistent route, designate a ward specialist, an emergency closure authority, a quarantine area, and an extraction plan.
