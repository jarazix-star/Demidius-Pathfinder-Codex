# Ring of Continuation Audit

**Rating:** S rules audit

**Target:** Demidius

**Source:** *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF p. 169 / printed p. 168.
**Verification:** Complete item text reviewed in context.

The ring can maintain one personal-range spell for 24 hours or until the wearer casts another personal-range spell. The exception for retaining the older spell applies only while its natural duration would still be running.

This may conflict with Demidius's all-day Invoke Deity (Luck) routine: after the spell's natural duration expires, casting another personal spell may end the ring-maintained effect. Record the campaign spell text, its natural duration, and the GM's interpretation before relying on the routine.
