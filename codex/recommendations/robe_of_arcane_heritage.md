# Robe of Arcane Heritage

**Rating:** A+ conditional

**Target:** Okeanikos or Tulip

**Source:** *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF p. 217 / printed p. 216.
**Verification:** Rule text reviewed in context.

The robe treats a sorcerer's class level as four higher for bloodline powers and their effects. It is a strong audit candidate for Okeanikos's draconic progression and Tulip's unicorn bloodline. Confirm their exact levels, bloodline wording, existing bonuses, and body-slot competition before purchase.
