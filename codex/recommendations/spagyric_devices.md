# Spagyric Devices

**Rating:** S-  
**Verification:** Verified source; GM approval essential  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Alchemists’ Discoveries*, PDF p. 4–6  
**Primary role:** Daily reconfigurable equipment pool

## Analysis

Replaces one major alchemy subsystem with a level-scaled pool of magic-item-like devices rebuilt and maintained daily. Because the devices are temporary, bulky, fragile, and creator-dependent, this is not ordinary wealth—but it can still equip the Dawnrunner for specific missions with extraordinary flexibility.
