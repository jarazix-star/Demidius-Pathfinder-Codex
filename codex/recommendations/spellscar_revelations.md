# Spellscar Revelations

**Rating:** A for Magic Penetration; B+ for Mystic Null  
**Priority:** Investigate legal access; not currently assumed available  
**Verification:** Printed revelations reviewed in context  
**Source:** *Inner Sea Magic* (PZO9237), PDF p. 32 / printed p. 30  
**Primary role:** Dispel reliability and magical defense

## Analysis

Magic Penetration improves caster-level checks made to dispel or remove magic and scales at higher oracle level. It is an unusually direct addition to Demidius's dispel engine. Mystic Null provides an insight bonus on saves against spells and spell-like abilities, later expanding to supernatural abilities; the mystery also offers scaling spell resistance.

## Access restriction

These are Spellscar mystery revelations. Demidius should not be treated as having access merely because they are useful. A legal revelation-sharing feat, explicit retraining permission, compatible archetype, or custom divine reward must be identified first.

## Disposition

Research access to Magic Penetration before considering Mystic Null. If no legal route exists, preserve both as design references for a campaign Boon or custom Hermes anti-magic gift rather than silently importing them.
