# Superior Tactician

**Rating:** A  
**Verification:** Verified source; officer feat  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Cavaliers’ Orders*, PDF p. 9  
**Primary role:** Teamwork-feat distribution

## Analysis

Improves tactician to a move action, or free action for a 9th-level cavalier, doubles range to 60 feet, and extends duration to 3 rounds +1/level. It is an exceptional action-economy upgrade for a teamwork-focused officer.
