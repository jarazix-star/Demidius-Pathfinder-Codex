# Wandering Weather

**Rating:** S

**Priority:** Take as soon as possible

**Verification:** Verified against the local *Ultimate Wilderness* PDF; d20PFSRD independently confirms the spell entry and Section 15 attribution
**Source:** *Ultimate Wilderness* (PZO1140), PDF p. 238 / printed p. 237; [d20PFSRD spell entry](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/w/wandering-weather/)
**Primary role:** Dawnrunner propulsion and strategic mobility

## Analysis

*Wandering weather* is an 8th-level cleric, druid, shaman, sorcerer/wizard, and witch spell. It functions as *control weather*, with the crucial option to keep the weather centered on the caster while the caster moves. That mobile center makes it suitable for a moving galleon in a way ordinary *control weather* is not.

The spell should be treated as an urgent acquisition for Demidius through the campaign's wizard-spell access ruling. Before assigning a numerical speed increase, confirm how the chosen seasonal weather and wind interact with the campaign vessel workbook and whether remaining "centered on you" is sufficient when Demidius is aboard a moving ship.

## Codex Disposition

Place *wandering weather* at the top of Demidius's near-term spell-acquisition list. The spell and its mobile-weather clause are verified. The projected speed increase remains a campaign calculation rather than a printed spell benefit.
