# Whispers

**Rating:** A  
**Verification:** Verified source; acquisition unresolved  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Witches’ Hexes*, PDF p. 4  
**Primary role:** Targeted skill supremacy

## Analysis

Grants half witch level to one selected skill, with duration based on the skill. It can reinforce Diplomacy, Spellcraft, Use Magic Device, crafting, Profession (sailor), or other decisive checks. It is less dramatic than Wish Giver but much easier to exploit repeatedly across the organization.
