# Wish Giver

**Rating:** S  
**Verification:** Verified source; acquisition unresolved  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Witches’ Hexes*, PDF p. 6  
**Primary role:** Strategic flexibility

## Analysis

Once per day grants another adjacent creature a limited wish without a material component, subject to listed restrictions and a 30-day recipient lockout. Even with those limits, this is campaign-scale flexibility and a premier candidate for a divine boon rather than a routine class pickup.
