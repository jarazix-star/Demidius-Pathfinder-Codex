# Influence Engine

Influence combines Charisma, diplomacy, leadership, divine reputation, social magic, political alliances, and loyalty relationships.

## Primary components

- High Charisma and the Eyebrow Piercing of Confidence.
- Epic Leadership and Dawnrunner command structure.
- High Clerist and other campaign-scale mythic options.
- Queen Lidda Beaumont's alliance and the Glasses of Beaumont.
- The demiplane's Loyalty Fatal Flaw, which must be treated as a responsibility and risk rather than ordinary mind control.

## Safeguard doctrine

Demidius's -20 vulnerability when judging the intentions of women or effeminate men requires independent review of important social conclusions. Major commitments should be checked by trusted allies, divinations, or objective evidence.
