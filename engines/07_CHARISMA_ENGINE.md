# Charisma Engine

Charisma drives oracle spell DCs, bonus spells, social skills, Leadership, Seven-Pipped Gem uses, and all Will saves under Steadfast Personality.

A +6 bonus to the **Charisma score** normally changes the Charisma modifier by +3, subject to the starting score's parity. Record score bonuses and modifier changes separately.
