# Aristea - Ranked Mythic Feats and Path Abilities

## Current build and ranking method

This guide is tailored to Aristea at level 17: Exploiter Wizard 9 / Crossblooded Wildblooded Sorcerer 1 / Arcane Trickster 7, effective wizard casting 16, 8th-level spells, Nereus devotee, Animal Lord of the Tides, primary crafter, shapeshifter, and water/cold ray striker. It assumes actual sneak attack +11d6: 6d6 from the sheet plus 5d6 from her artifact.

It also respects the campaign rulings that each qualifying ray can apply sneak attack and that Benthic Spell, Merciful Spell, Sap Adept, and Sap Master form an intended nonlethal damage engine. Sap Master still requires the target to be truly flat-footed unless the GM states otherwise. Continuing Arcane Trickster through level 20 remains the recommended class plan.

Ranks measure value to this build, not generic power. S is build-defining, A is consistently excellent, B is useful or situational, C is narrow or redundant, D is a poor use of a scarce selection, and F conflicts with the build. Options tagged **Current** are already represented in the current project records.

## Executive recommendation

Aristea's best mythic engine is **Wild Arcana**, **Eldritch Breach**, **Arcane Metamastery**, **Channel Power**, **Seeking Spell (Mythic)**, and **Spell Penetration (Mythic)**. This combination gives her the right spell on demand, makes it land, and multiplies the value of her enormous sneak-attack payload. Her mythic selections should improve access, accuracy, positioning, and action economy before adding small amounts of damage.

### Suggested acquisition order

1. Wild Arcana.
2. Extra Path Ability, if needed to obtain Wild Arcana or Eldritch Breach.
3. Eldritch Breach.
4. Spell Penetration (Mythic).
5. Seeking Spell (Mythic), with GM approval for the Legendary Games feat.
6. Arcane Metamastery.
7. Piercing Spell (Mythic) or Extra Mythic Power.
8. Channel Power.
9. Arcanist's Escape or Mirror Dodge.
10. Mythic Spellpower.

## Practical loadout by scene

- **Single apex target:** Eldritch Breach, Spell Penetration (Mythic), Piercing Spell (Mythic), Seeking Spell (Mythic), and a multi-ray spell.
- **Nonlethal takedown:** Arcane Metamastery with Benthic and Merciful Spell, plus confirmed flat-footed setup for Sap Master.
- **Crowded ship combat:** Spell Sieve, Energy Conversion, Rime Spell (Mythic), and an area water or cold spell.
- **Unknown expedition:** Wild Arcana, Perfect Preparation, Rapid Preparation, Planar Scholar, and Contingency Plan.
- **Personal survival:** Mirror Dodge or Arcanist's Escape, Resilient Arcana, Iron Will (Mythic), and Great Fortitude (Mythic).
