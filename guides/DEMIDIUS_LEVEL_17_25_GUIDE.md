# Demidius Thorne

## Level 17-25 Optimization and Advancement Guide

Version 1.0 - 22 July 2026

### Executive recommendation

Demidius should remain a Charisma-first Spirit Guide Oracle whose best combat contribution is removing enemy magic, protecting the party's action economy, and turning one successful dispel into a full-party opening. He should not spend scarce feats or godly powers trying to compete with the party's damage specialists.

The immediate order is:

1. Preserve Mythic Greater Dispel Magic and Spell Perfection (greater dispel magic).
2. Fill any missing pieces of the dispel chain: Dispel Synergy, its mythic version, and reliable initiative.
3. Reach Oracle 18 for 9th-level spells.
4. Obtain wandering weather and secure a GM ruling for its effect on the Dawnrunner.
5. Confirm whether Evangelist's Aligned Class advances Oracle beyond 20 before entering the prestige class.
6. Use the first inexpensive campaign-Boon mythic-feat purchases on Dispel Synergy (Mythic) and Improved Initiative (Mythic), if their prerequisites are met.
7. After the cheap mythic-feat purchases, save toward Mortal God unless a Suzerain-template upgrade produces a clearly more urgent campaign-scale benefit.

## Recommended class progression

### Primary track - if Evangelist is approved before level 20

| Character level | Class level | Effective Oracle level | Main gain and action |
|---:|---|---:|---|
| 17 | Oracle 17 | 17 | Current state. Audit feats, revelations, mythic abilities, and spells known. |
| 18 | Oracle 18 | 18 | Gain 9th-level Oracle spell access. Lock miracle as the first broad answer unless already covered. |
| 19 | Evangelist 1 | 18 | Pay the one-level Aligned Class delay only after 9th-level spells are secured. Confirm Hermes obedience package first. |
| 20 | Evangelist 2 | 19 | Aligned Class resumes Oracle advancement. Confirm how epic spell slots will work before proceeding. |
| 21 | Evangelist 3 | 20 | Reach effective Oracle 20 and the final revelation if Aligned Class advances it. Evangelist boon milestone is redundant if the custom Hermes package already grants the same benefit. |
| 22 | Evangelist 4 | 21 | Continue only if the GM has defined Oracle 21+ advancement. |
| 23 | Evangelist 5 | 22 | Continue the Oracle engine through Aligned Class. |
| 24 | Evangelist 6 | 23 | Second Evangelist boon milestone; choose a nonduplicative Hermes benefit. |

This is the best thematic track only if the GM confirms all four points: Evangelist is legal in epic play, Aligned Class advances Oracle after 20, the level-19 delay does not block the epic spell table, and the custom Hermes obedience has nonduplicative boons.

### Safe fallback track

Take Oracle 18, Oracle 19, and Oracle 20 at character levels 18-20. This preserves the published Oracle endpoint without interpretation. At level 21, enter a full-casting epic or prestige progression only after the table defines level 10 and 11 spell slots. Exalted is the strongest divine-obedience theme; Loremaster is the cleanest neutral full-casting chassis; Harrower is the most thematic luck chassis. None automatically creates epic spell slots.

## Standard feat plan

### Retraining order

Apply these only if the named feat is still present and no current story arc needs it.

| Priority | Retrain | Replace with | Reason |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Deceitful | Dispel Synergy | Converts a successful dispel into a saving-throw opening. |
| 2 | Conceal Spell | Improved Initiative | Acting before the enemy caster is worth more than routine concealment. |
| 3 | Divine Fighting Technique | Destructive Dispel | The rapier is a backup; the stun/sicken rider supports the real combat engine. |
| 4 | Diverse Obedience | Inexplicable Luck or Divine Interference | Retrain only if the custom Hermes system fully replaces its unique boon access. |

### New feat slot queue

Demidius receives ordinary character feats at levels 19, 21, 23, and 25. At each slot, take the first legal missing option in this order.

1. Dispel Synergy.
2. Improved Initiative.
3. Destructive Dispel.
4. Divine Interference - expend a divine spell to force an enemy to reroll a successful attack against an ally.
5. Inexplicable Luck - prerequisite for the excellent mythic version.
6. Persistent Spell - strongest general save-pressure metamagic when immunity has already been removed.
7. Piercing Spell - conditional third-party prerequisite for its mythic version when spell resistance is a recurring obstacle.
8. Augment Teleportation - conditional Super Genius Games option for fleet and extraction logistics after GM approval.

Do not spend a new feat on weapon damage. Do not retrain Spell Perfection, its metamagic prerequisites, Fate's Favored, Strange Revelation, or any feat the current dispel equation actually uses.

## Mythic path ability order

First audit the sheet for Inspired Spell, Eldritch Breach, Divine Metamastery, Enduring Blessing, and Flexible Counterspell. Fill missing core abilities before adding luxuries.

| Priority | Mythic path ability | Function |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Inspired Spell | Cast any eligible divine-list spell without knowing or preparing it; the campaign's wizard-as-divine ruling may make this enormous. |
| 2 | Eldritch Breach | Roll twice on caster-level checks to dispel, penetrate spell resistance, or determine whether magic affects a target. |
| 3 | Divine Metamastery | Apply key metamagic without slot or casting-time increase; build toward Quicken. |
| 4 | Enduring Blessing | Convert important long buffs into 24-hour effects. A second selection at tier 6 reaches shorter buffs. |
| 5 | Make Your Own Luck | Legendary Games option that converts a competence, insight, or morale bonus into luck for the campaign's Fate's Favored interaction. |
| 6 | Contingency Plan | Mythic Minis option for action-efficient contingency access and emergency planning. |

Cross-path and third-party selections require the campaign's normal approval route. High Clerist is strategically exceptional, but it should not displace the core casting engine before tier 6.

## Deific Obedience - Hermes

Deific Obedience boons are not the campaign's spendable Boons, and neither system replaces the trials that made Demidius Blessed of Hermes.

The campaign permits a custom package assembled from published obedience boons, provided every selection remains in its original tier and fits one of Hermes's recorded domains: Trickery, Travel, Community, Liberation, or Luck. The domain-constrained ranking is recorded in `../research/DEIFIC_OBEDIENCE_DOMAIN_RANKINGS.md`.

| Boon tier | Recommended Hermes boon | Printed model | Advice |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Lucky Blessing | Chaldira, Exalted 1 | Luck-domain spell-like abilities, including *divine favor* 3/day and *prayer* 1/day. |
| 2 | Fortune's Child - already acquired | Chaldira, Sentinel 2 | Increases all received luck bonuses and anchors the current probability engine. |
| 3 | Seven-Pipped Gem - already acquired | Nivi Rhombodazzle, Exalted 3 | The defining post-roll Luck-domain intervention. |

If acquired Hermes gifts remain separate from obedience slots, use Lucky Blessing / Fortunate Spells / Blast of Motes. Invoke Uprising is the preferred tier-3 substitution when hostile mind control is the greatest threat. The previous Zura Charisma recommendation is excluded because it does not fit a recorded Hermes domain. Do not purchase Fortune's Child or Seven-Pipped Gem a second time.

## Spendable campaign Boons

Use this order unless a story event changes the goal.

1. Buy Dispel Synergy (Mythic) if its current Boon cost is one or two and the base feat is complete.
2. Buy Improved Initiative (Mythic) as the second inexpensive mythic-feat purchase if legal.
3. Confirm the exact tier of the Suzerain template. Its enhanced distribution already reaches every ally within 250 feet, with no fixed recipient cap. If a three- or five-Boon upgrade materially expands that organizational reach, compare it against the Mortal God fund.
4. Save 10 Boons to advance from demigod to Mortal God. This is the default long-term target because a divine-rank increase affects multiple campaign systems.
5. Bank a small reserve for a god save, divine negotiation, or emergency favor during the Culling.

Do not buy adoption without clarifying whether it would alter the existing Aphrodite ancestry or Hermes patron relationship. Do not spend expensive late mythic-feat purchases on small numerical bonuses when the Mortal God threshold is within reach.

## Nonmythic spell acquisition order

### Immediate - levels 17-18

1. Wandering weather - highest-priority fleet spell; its center follows the caster. Obtain a GM ruling for the actual Dawnrunner speed calculation.
2. Greater dispel magic - keep known and ready as the signature spell.
3. Blessing of fervor - premier party action, mobility, and defense buff.
4. Death ward and freedom of movement - prevent common high-level shutdowns.
5. Greater heroism - long-duration competence support for a key ally.
6. Contingency - emergency extraction, survival, or anti-control trigger.
7. Forcecage - no-save battlefield partition after the opening dispel.
8. Mind blank - essential defense and the protection Demidius must expect to remove from enemies.
9. Moment of prescience - decisive insight bonus when Seven-Pipped Gem must be conserved.

### First 9th-level spells

1. Miracle - broadest emergency and campaign-scale answer.
2. Overwhelming presence - mass encounter control after mental defenses fall.
3. Gate - planar access and extraction; calling uses remain dangerous and expensive.
4. Time stop - preparation and tempo, not direct offense.
5. Mage's disjunction - reserve for situations where collateral damage is acceptable.
6. True resurrection - only after a trapped soul has been released.

### Strategic and infrastructure additions

- Permanency and mythic permanency for protected infrastructure.
- Forbiddance and dimensional lock for the demiplane, temples, and secure rooms.
- Greater teleport and teleportation circle for fleet and organization logistics.
- Discern location, commune, legend lore, and contact other plane for intelligence.
- Mage's magnificent mansion for expedition support.
- Billow the sail only after the GM approves Frog God Games material and the workbook multiplication order.
- Calculated luck before a planned sequence of caster-level checks; Seven-Pipped Gem remains better for one pivotal check.

## Combat doctrine

1. Before the fight, identify enemy protections and activate long-duration defenses.
2. Win initiative; do not spend Seven-Pipped Gem on a low-impact roll.
3. Open with targeted Mythic Greater Dispel Magic against mind blank, freedom of movement, spell turning, death ward, contingencies, and escape effects.
4. Apply Dispel Synergy and let the party exploit the saving-throw penalty.
5. Use no-save force control when immunity remains; use enchantment only after mental defenses are actually removed.
6. Keep an immediate action for counterspelling or emergency protection.
7. Use Amazing Initiative or other extra actions only when the extra spell changes the encounter.
