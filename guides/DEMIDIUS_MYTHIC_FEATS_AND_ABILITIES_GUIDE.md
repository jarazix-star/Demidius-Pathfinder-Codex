# Demidius Thorne - Ranked Mythic Feats and Path Abilities

## Current build and ranking method

This guide is tailored to Demidius at level 17: Spirit Guide oracle, Suzerain, demigod of Aphrodite, Blessed of Hermes, primary captain, controller, dispeller, buffer, face, and probability manipulator. It assumes Charisma 66, Profession (sailor) +113, mythic rank 7, 21 mythic power per day, mythic *greater dispel magic*, and the campaign rule that wizard spells Demidius learns are cast as divine spells.

Ranks measure value to this build, not generic power. S is build-defining, A is consistently excellent, B is useful or situational, C is narrow or redundant, D is a poor use of a scarce selection, and F conflicts with the build. Options tagged **Current** are already shown on the current sheet. They are graded honestly rather than treated as new selections.

The campaign formulas matter: every mythic feat adds 1 mythic power, and every two path abilities add a mythic tier. That makes Extra Path Ability and Extra Mythic Power unusually important. Confirm the exact current path-ability count before spending the next boon or tier selection.

## Executive recommendation

The strongest immediate package is **Inspired Spell**, **Eldritch Breach**, **Divine Metamastery**, **Dispel Synergy (Mythic)**, **Extra Mythic Power**, and **Make Your Own Luck**. After that, add action insurance and duration through **Contingency Plan**, **Flexible Counterspell**, and **Enduring Blessing**. Demidius should spend mythic resources to decide encounters, not merely increase damage.

### Suggested acquisition order

1. Dispel Synergy (Mythic), if the GM approves the Legendary Games feat.
2. Inspired Spell.
3. Eldritch Breach.
4. Extra Mythic Power.
5. Divine Metamastery.
6. Make Your Own Luck, after GM review.
7. Contingency Plan.
8. Enduring Blessing.
9. Improved Initiative (Mythic).
10. Flexible Counterspell or High Clerist.

## Practical loadout by scene

- **Apex caster fight:** Eldritch Breach, Dispel Synergy (Mythic), Flexible Counterspell, Improved Counterspell (Mythic), and mythic *greater dispel magic*.
- **Unknown divine crisis:** Inspired Spell, Divine Metamastery, Extra Mythic Power, and Contingency Plan.
- **Fleet or crew action:** Enduring Blessing, Caster's Friend, Loyalty, and Surge of Inspiration.
- **Planar rescue or Tartarus:** Planar Scholar, Planar Savant, Inspired Spell, and Farwalker.
- **Court or divine negotiation:** Make Your Own Luck, Inexplicable Luck (Mythic), Display of Charisma, and Persuasive (Mythic).

## Rules and source notes

- **Paizo** means *Pathfinder Roleplaying Game: Mythic Adventures* or a Paizo mythic feat referenced by the local rules corpus.
- **Legendary Games** and **Super Genius Games** are third-party options and require GM approval unless already approved in this campaign.
- The rankings use the current local character sheet and project rulings. They do not assume that an option is legal merely because it appears in a third-party book.
- Reconcile the sheet's exact number of godly powers, path abilities, mythic feats, and boon-purchased feats before applying the next tier or mythic-power total.
