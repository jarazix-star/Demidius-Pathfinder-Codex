# Super Genius Games — High-Value Index

This index covers the first four completed Advanced Options extractions.

- **Navigator** (S) — *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 7: Cuts voyage time or encounter risk, improves crew initiative/awareness, and grants ship-related rerolls.
- **Wish Giver** (S) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 6: Grant another creature a limited wish once/day without material component.
- **Doom Gaze** (S-) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 4: Penalty to AC, checks, attacks, saves, or skills; scales to –8 at 16th.
- **Mass Production** (S-) — *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF p. 6: Duplicate devices count at half and then quarter cost against the daily device limit.
- **Spagyric Devices** (S-) — *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF p. 4: Replace bombs, mutagen, or extracts with a daily reconfigurable pool of magic-item-like devices based on a level-scaled gp limit.
- **Mock** (A+) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 5: Staggers a target for level rounds, with repeated saves and once/day targeting.
- **Rune Hex** (A+) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 5: Stores a known hex in a touch/read-triggered rune.
- **Retainers** (A+) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 6: Leadership-like retainers, adding Intelligence to score and replacing cohort with multiple special retainers.
- **Quicken Hex** (A+) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 6: Use a hex as a swift action once/day; later works on a major hex.
- **Esoteric Studies** (A+) — *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF p. 6: Select two spells per class level from any list to expand item prerequisites for devices.
- **Ship's Officer** (A) — *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 2: Inspire courage plus ship-wide pennant/banner support.
- **Crown Privateer** (A) — *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 6: Expands paladin auras and sacred defenses to all allies aboard the same ship.
- **Addict** (A*) — *Advanced Options: Oracle's Curses*, PDF p. 2: Drug dependency; lower-level oracle spells use the highest available spell level when determining save DC.
- **Drunkard** (A*) — *Advanced Options: Oracle's Curses*, PDF p. 2: Alcohol creates stacking Wisdom-check penalties and Charisma-check bonuses up to half oracle level.
- **Whispers** (A) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 4: Grants half witch level to one skill, with duration based on the skill.
- **Ally** (A) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 4: Material-component-free lesser planar ally, upgrading to planar ally.
- **Allure** (A) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 5: Sympathy centered on the witch.
- **Shapeshift** (A) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 6: Shapechange without focus for 5 minutes/level per day.
- **Hex Expertise** (A) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 6: Use a selected once-per-24-hours hex twice on the same target.
- **Hex Focus** (A) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 6: Increase one selected hex's save DC by 2.
- **Ultimate Metamorph** (A) — *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF p. 8: Once/day polymorph any object.
- **Seaswain** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 5: Charisma-based nautical monk with weapon flurry, surface-running, execution strike, and elemental transformation.
- **Lookout** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF p. 8: Improves long-range Perception and exposes illusions with extra saves and ally support.
- **Provocative** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Oracle's Curses*, PDF p. 4: Nonlethal vulnerability in exchange for a large Diplomacy bonus and accelerated attitude influence.
- **Cornucopia** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 4: Enhanced provisions; later long-duration heroes' feast.
- **Kiss of Oblivion** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF p. 5: Modify memory through a kiss.
- **Blinding Bomb** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF p. 2: Replaces bomb damage with permanent blindness on direct hit and 1-round splash blindness.
- **Homunculus** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF p. 3: Gain a homunculus familiar, replaceable for 1,000 gp and one day.
- **Phantom Mutagen** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF p. 3: Mutagen grants greater invisibility at class level.
- **Potent Extract** (A-) — *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF p. 4: Treat extract caster level as 2 higher.
