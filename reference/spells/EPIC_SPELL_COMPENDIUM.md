# Epic Spells

This section preserves the campaign's custom level 10 and level 11 arcane and divine spells from `Epic_Spell_Compendium_Second_Pass.docx`. The source is a second-pass transcription and conservative Pathfinder 1e stat-block reconstruction prepared from five handwritten campaign-reference pages.

The compendium contains **58 unique spells** represented by **78 source-list entries**. Shared arcane/divine spells are consolidated below because their repeated stat blocks are identical.

> **Verification policy:** Directly visible numbers and effects are preserved. Fields inherited from a published base spell are labeled **reconstructed**. Fields without a safe basis remain **not specified**. Suggested or ambiguous material still requires GM confirmation.

## Campaign integration

The campaign supports post-20 and mythic progression. Demidius may cast wizard spells as divine spells under campaign rule CR-09, but actual access to these level 10-11 spells still depends on the campaign's spell-slot, learning, preparation, and approval rules.

## Important transcription notes

- Death Stare appears to be an inserted “14A” entry between Divine Name and Hell Fireball.
- The Firestorm area notation is interpreted as two 10-foot cubes per caster level, matching PF1e fire storm notation.
- Epic Particulate Form reads 20 minutes/level on the arcane page. The divine page appears to read 40 minutes/level, but the first digit is partly obscured.
- Mass Dominate Monster is crossed out on the level 11 arcane page but is clearly present on the level 11 divine page.
- Epic Finger of Death contains two separate save clauses; its exact resolution needs GM confirmation.
