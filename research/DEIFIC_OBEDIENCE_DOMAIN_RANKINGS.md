# Domain-Constrained Deific Obedience Rankings

The durable master analysis is maintained in the local Project store as
`DEIFIC_OBEDIENCE_RESEARCH.md`.

This repository copy records the current conclusions for the character guides and generated PDFs.

## Eligibility

- Hermes: Trickery, Travel, Community, Liberation, or Luck.
- Nereus: Knowledge, Shapeshifting, or Sea/Water, pending final GM confirmation of his formal campaign domain list.

## Demidius

- Tier 1 top choice: Chaldira Exalted 1, Lucky Blessing.
- Tier 2 top choice: Chaldira Sentinel 2, Fortune's Child (already acquired).
- Tier 3 top choice: Nivi Rhombodazzle Exalted 3, Seven-Pipped Gem (already acquired).
- If acquired gifts remain separate from obedience slots: Lucky Blessing / Fortunate Spells / Blast of Motes.
- Zura's Charisma boon is excluded because it does not fit Hermes's domains.

## Aristea

- Existing reward: Animal Lord of the Tides, graded S but separate from published obedience tiers.
- Tier 1 top choice: Brigh Exalted 1, Creator, when Aristea remains the primary crafter; otherwise Otolmens 1, Discerning Mind.
- Tier 2 top choice: Lymnieris Celestial Mystery Cultist 2, Rite of Passage, for permanent-duration *greater polymorph* on one willing creature.
- Tier 3 top choice for campaign economy: Torag Evangelist 3, Sacred Crafting, reducing magic-item base cost, crafting time, and final price by 10% after calculations.
- Tier 3 top choice for fleet operations: Besmara Exalted 3, Rally Crew. At level 17 it gives every ally within 60 feet +10 feet of speed and *heroism* for 17 hours, while benefited rolls ignore water- and weather-based penalties.
- Tier 3 top choice for combat: Haagenti Sentinel 3, Adaptive Flesh and Twisting Steel.
- Brigh Exalted 3, Inspired Crafting, is the second crafting option and doubles productive progress during a four-hour adventuring-day work period.
- Combat-caster package: Discerning Mind / Poisoned Mysticism / Adaptive Flesh and Twisting Steel.
- Lore-first package: Secret Lore / Borrowed Memories / River's Embodiment.
- Tanagaar's Hunter's Edge and Andirifkhu's Subtle Razor are excluded because sneak attack does not fit Nereus's domains.

See the master analysis for ten or more ranked Aristea choices at every tier, grades, source tracks, and GM questions.
