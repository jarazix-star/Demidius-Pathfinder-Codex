# Legendary Games Deep Dive: Demidius and Aristea

## Scope

The local Legendary Games library contains **219 PDFs**. Earlier repository work already completed the two major spell compilations and six Mythic Minis, while *Mythic Hero's Handbook* and *Metamagic Feats* had focused but incomplete coverage.

This pass examined **18 books / 122 PDF pages** selected for Demidius's oracle, luck, dispel, teleportation, Leadership, mythic-resource, social, divine, and legendary-item engines and Aristea's ray, ranged-touch, damage, metamagic, and crafting engines. Every selected PDF was text-extracted. The cited rules pages were rendered and visually checked for page numbering, columns, and continuations.

This is a content-level targeted pass, not a claim that all 219 PDFs are fully extracted. The 15 short Mythic Minis are completely cataloged below. *Mythic Skills*, *Past Lives*, and *Ultimate Relationships* received focused system extraction of the sections relevant to the two characters.

All citations use PDF page numbers. Mechanical text is paraphrased.

## Best new candidates for Demidius

1. **Soul Safe** - a legendary item with eternal bond that is also a minor or major artifact can reform its owner 24 hours after death and can spend legendary power to negate death effects or energy drain. Demidius's artifact/legendary-item combination makes this the most important discovery in the pass. It does not expressly defeat soul trapping. *Legendary Item Abilities*, PDF p. 2.
2. **Abundant Revelations (Mythic)** - adds uses of the revelation selected with Abundant Revelations equal to half mythic tier and allows the selected revelation to be changed during the daily spell-refresh meditation by spending mythic power. *Divine Feats*, PDF p. 2.
3. **Ring of Returning** - creates a reliable return route after teleportation; a mythic wearer has an hour-long window, can bring original passengers back, can extend the window, and can attempt to overcome nonmythic teleportation blocks. *Mythic Rings*, PDF p. 2.
4. **Expert Aid** - extends aid another to any threatened creature, increases its bonus by half tier, and adds tier to the enabling attack roll. This is direct party-enablement technology. *Marshal Path Abilities*, PDF p. 2.
5. **Shatter Resistance** - after hitting a creature, spend mythic power to reduce its spell resistance by tier for one minute. This is strong for a support attacker if Demidius can access Marshal abilities. *Marshal Path Abilities*, PDF p. 2.
6. **Healing Surge** - adds the surge die to adjacent-ally healing or temporary hp and can extend to multi-target healing with mythic power. *Marshal Path Abilities II*, PDF p. 2.
7. **High Clerist** - provides Leadership or Mythic Leadership, religious construction discounts, and a crusade/follower engine. This is an infrastructure choice, not a combat default. *Hierophant Path Abilities*, PDF p. 2.
8. **Piercing Spell (Mythic)** - treats enemy spell resistance as 5 + tier lower against nonmythic foes or 5 + half tier lower against mythic foes; can also spend mythic power for the base metamagic without slot adjustment. *Metamagic Feats*, PDF p. 2.
9. **Echoing Spell (Mythic)** - after casting the echoing spell, gain one same-day use as an SLA without verbal, somatic, or material components. Excellent for a pivotal control or recovery spell if the prerequisite feat is already worthwhile. *Metamagic Feats*, PDF p. 2.
10. **Mythic Spellcraft: Unravel Magic** - spend three mythic power to use touch-range single-target dispel magic as a supernatural ability, with a Spellcraft-rank/tier caster-level formula and advantage on a surge used for the dispel check. *Mythic Skills*, PDF p. 18.
11. **Mythic Bluff: Tell a Lie** - adds tier as an insight bonus when lying to nonmythic creatures and applies to convincing charmed/suggested creatures that a service is reasonable. This is mechanically powerful but magnifies Demidius's existing social/fatal-flaw risk. *Mythic Skills*, PDF p. 11.
12. **Resonant Regalia** - splits one legendary item bond across a set, adds a resonant power, improves legendary surge when the set is worn, and can expand at higher tier. Extremely powerful but requires explicit GM design approval. *Legendary Item Abilities*, PDF p. 2.

## Best new candidates for Aristea

1. **Seeking Spell (Mythic)** - works with spells requiring ranged attacks or ranged touch attacks and strips cover bonuses from Reflex saves on area spells; also permits on-demand base Seeking Spell with mythic power. This is the cleanest direct match for a ray specialist. *Metamagic Feats*, PDF p. 3.
2. **Piercing Spell (Mythic)** - substantial spell-resistance reduction for damaging rays and other offensive spells. *Metamagic Feats*, PDF p. 2.
3. **Spell Dilation** - adjusts one dimension of a spell area by 5 feet, plus more at higher tier, within a 50% limit. This is better for area control than single-target rays but broadens Aristea's battlefield role. *Archmage Path Abilities*, PDF p. 2.
4. **Rod of Spell Focusing** - attunes to one school or acid/cold/electricity/fire, grants the corresponding focus feat, upgrades to the mythic focus feat if the wielder already has the base feat, and provides limited school/energy detection. *Mythic Rods*, PDF p. 2.
5. **Orb of the Seventh Star** - provides seven daily magic-missile motes, can add two to another magic-missile effect, and for a mythic arcane caster can hold seven levels of low-level spell preparation/slots and mythic-enhance orb-created missile effects. *Mythic Slotless Items*, PDF p. 2.
6. **Diamond of Everwinter** - strong fire/cold protection, free Rime Spell activation with mythic power, arctic transport, and additional powers as the owner gains tiers. Particularly good if Aristea uses cold rays. *Mythic Jewels*, PDF p. 2.
7. **Flaring Spell (Mythic)** - damaged creatures save or become blinded for one round and dazzled afterward. Adds a control rider to direct damage. *Metamagic Feats*, PDF p. 2.
8. **Toppling Spell (Mythic)** - improves the trip check from Toppling Spell and creates stacking bonuses when a multi-target force spell trips adjacent foes. High value only for a force/magic-missile package. *Metamagic Feats*, PDF p. 3.
9. **Arcanist's Escape** - high-tier free-action dimension door when entangled, grappled, or pinned, with an expensive option to bypass nonmythic teleport blocks. *Archmage Path Abilities*, PDF p. 2.
10. **Mythic Use Magic Device** - can activate known or qualifying spell-trigger effects as mythic spells, providing a route to mythic effects through staves and other crafted gear. *Mythic Skills*, PDF p. 21.

### Archmage Path Abilities II

Source file: `Mythic Minis/Mythic Minis 36 - Archmage Path Abilities II.pdf`; all rules PDF p. 2.

The book is mainly alchemist, witch, magus, sorcerer, and polymorph support: **Cackling Chorus, Purging Alchemy, Repeating Arcana, Battlemagic Sense, Blighted Alchemy, Bloodline Transfusion, Multimorph,** and **Greater Blighted Alchemy**. ### Feats of Miscellaneous Magic

### Metamagic Feats

Source file: `Mythic Minis/Mythic Minis 51 - Metamagic Feats.pdf`; rules PDF pp. 2-3. This completes the prior focused extraction.

Complete mythic feat list: **Burning, Coaxing, Concussive, Consecrate, Echoing, Flaring, Jinxed, Piercing, Rime, Seeking, Shadow Grasp, Tenebrous, Thanatopic, Threatening Illusion, Threnodic, Toppling,** and **Umbral Spell**.

### Inherent Advancement

Source file: `Mythic Minis/Mythic Minis 80 - Inherent Advancement.pdf`; system rules PDF p. 2.

This is a campaign-wide alternate progression that replaces much of the expected “Big Six” item economy with level-based selections. Options include ability enhancements, caster-level/concentration competence, weapon/armor/save/natural-armor bonuses, skill enhancement, extra feats, insight AC, luck to saves, inherent ability bonuses, and extra spells known.

It should not be added piecemeal. If adopted, Demidius would strongly value **Competent Casting, Lucky, Perfected Ability,** and **Spell Knowledge**, while Aristea would value **Competent Casting, Enhanced Skill, Extra Feat, Perfected Ability,** and **Spell Knowledge**. The system would require a complete campaign wealth and item audit.

### Past Lives

Source file: `Past Lives.pdf`; system PDF pp. 6-7, incarnation feats pp. 21-23.

The system gives a GM-secret past-life profile whose benefits and drawbacks unfold at level/story milestones. It is intentionally net-positive but adds story obligations. All past-life characters suffer a resurrection complication: raise dead, true resurrection, and breath of life require a DC 20 caster-level check, reduced by the subject's Charisma bonus, because the reincarnating soul may move on.

This is **not recommended as a retrofit** for Demidius or Aristea without a story-first decision. For Aristea specifically, the system does not solve her existing Tartarus imprisonment and could add another soul-return complication. Its incarnation feats and magic items are profile-locked rather than general build options.

### Ultimate Relationships

Source file: `Ultimate Relationships.pdf`; core rules PDF pp. 8-11.

The system tracks rank 0-10 relationships through camaraderie points, milestones, rank-up checks, affinities, failures that improve later attempts, and possible rivalry conversion. Mechanical rewards include XP, custom boons, NPC participation, and eventual cohort availability (typically participation at rank 4 and cohort status at rank 7).

This is a strong campaign/influence subsystem for Demidius because it allows relationship progression through varied checks rather than only Diplomacy, while still rewarding his broad Charisma engine. It should be adopted as a campaign rule and used to formalize recurring NPC connections, not mined for isolated bonuses.

## Ruling and compatibility queue

1. **Soul Safe versus soul trapping:** it prevents permanent death and reforms the body, but does not state that it retrieves an imprisoned soul. Do not use it as an automatic Aristea rescue.
2. **Demidius's legendary item:** confirm it has or may acquire eternal bond and whether its artifact status meets Soul Safe.
3. **Path access:** confirm Demidius's current mythic path and legal routes to Marshal/Hierophant abilities; confirm Aristea's path before recommending Archmage powers.
4. **Abundant Revelations:** identify the current revelation chosen by the base feat and verify whether the campaign permits daily reselection of the mythic feat's target.
5. **Seeking Spell and rays:** the feat explicitly supports ranged attacks and ranged touch attacks, so it fits rays mechanically; confirm how it interacts with Aristea's campaign-specific ray/sneak-attack rule.
6. **Ring of Returning:** define whether Demidius can use its origin-return function across planar boundaries whenever the triggering teleport could do so.
7. **Mythic Skills:** decide the acquisition method and number of exploits; do not grant the whole book's skill benefits automatically.
8. **Relationship system:** decide whether ranks are per PC and NPC, and how existing Loyalty from the inherited demiplane interacts with camaraderie/affinity.
9. **Inherent Advancement:** campaign-wide replacement only; requires wealth, crafting, and existing-item conversion rules.
10. **Third-party overlap:** where an option also appears in *Mythic Hero's Handbook*, use the later/complete wording selected by the campaign and record the source choice.
