# Pathfinder Roleplaying Game Library: Content-Level Review

## Scope and method

This pass recursively indexed all 48 PDFs in `Pathfinder/Roleplaying Game`: 6,570 PDF pages, 6,444 pages with extractable text, and 25,892,930 extracted characters. The corpus includes the PF1 Core Rulebook, major rule hardcovers through *Occult Adventures* and *Bestiary 5*, the Beginner Box and supplements, the Harrow Deck, and reference products. Image-only maps, cards, and folio pages were cataloged even when they contained no extractable text.

Every source was searched at page level. High-value passages were then reviewed in context, and representative rules pages were rendered for visual verification. The extracted rulebook text and rendered pages remain in ignored temporary research storage. This repository contains only metadata, citations, summaries, and original campaign analysis.

The campaign's recorded rules remain authoritative. Optional systems from *Ultimate Campaign*, *Pathfinder Unchained*, and *Occult Adventures* are not active unless the GM adopts them. A printed option is not assumed legal merely because it is useful.

## Highest-value findings

### 1. Mnemonic vestment may radically expand Demidius's daily access

A mnemonic vestment lets a spontaneous caster spend a spell slot once per day to cast a spell from a carried written source as though the caster knew it. The spell must be on the caster's spell list, no higher than the slot, and the same arcane/divine type as the slot.

Under CR-09, Demidius can cast wizard spells as divine spells. If that ruling also treats those wizard spells as being on his usable class list, a 5,000 gp mnemonic vestment plus a curated spellbook becomes an extremely efficient once-per-day answer library. If CR-09 changes only how specific spells are cast and does not alter the list requirement, the vestment cannot provide this broader access.

**Source:** *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF p. 217 / printed p. 216.

**Priority:** S+ conditional. Obtain a direct GM ruling before purchase.

### 2. Otherworldly kimono directly improves dispel and spell-resistance checks

The otherworldly kimono grants a +4 untyped bonus on all caster-level checks and +4 resistance on saves. Once per day it can place a creature within 60 feet into a maze-like extradimensional prison without an initial save; while a victim remains trapped, both bonuses rise to +6. The caster-level bonus directly supports dispels, counterspells that require caster-level checks, and checks to overcome spell resistance.

**Source:** *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF pp. 217–218 / printed pp. 216–217.

**Priority:** S, subject to body-slot conflicts and campaign wealth.

### 3. Bead of karma is a powerful daily caster-level accelerator

The bead of karma in a standard or greater strand of prayer beads raises the wearer's caster level by 4 for 10 minutes and can be activated by a divine caster. It can strengthen a concentrated dispel/counterspell sequence, duration-dependent preparation, and other caster-level checks. It does not grant higher-level spell slots or spells known.

**Source:** *Core Rulebook* (PZO1110), PDF pp. 531–532 / printed pp. 530–531.

**Priority:** S for scheduled magical-supremacy encounters.

### 4. The Ring of Continuation has a consequential replacement rule

The ring extends a personal-range spell to 24 hours or until the wearer casts another personal-range spell, whichever comes first. A new personal spell does not eliminate the earlier one while the earlier spell's normal duration is still running, but after that natural duration expires the new personal spell replaces the ring-maintained effect. Dispel attempts work normally.

This means the recorded all-day *invoke deity (luck)* routine may restrict Demidius from safely casting later personal-range spells. The exact campaign version and the table's interpretation must be recorded before treating the effect as maintenance-free.

**Source:** *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF p. 169 / printed p. 168.

### 5. Four-leaf clover is efficient probability support under existing rulings

A four-leaf clover is a slotless, 3,750 gp item usable three times per day as a free action, even outside its owner's turn, to gain +2 luck on one ability check, saving throw, or skill check. For Demidius, CR-01 and CR-02 would raise a qualifying +2 luck bonus to +4. Ordinary same-type stacking still applies, so it replaces rather than stacks with his persistent luckstone bonus on the same roll.

**Source:** *Occult Adventures* (PZO1132), PDF p. 259 / printed p. 258.

**Priority:** A+: a cheap improvement on pivotal eligible rolls when Seven-Pipped Gem is unnecessary or its immediate action must be preserved.

### 6. Borrow Fortune is an emergency oracle reroll with a serious aftershock

*Borrow fortune* is an oracle 3 immediate-action spell that rerolls the caster's d20 before success or failure is known and keeps the better result. For the next two rounds, every d20 is rolled twice and uses the worse result. It can rescue a decisive check, but its following penalty can cripple saves, dispels, and other actions.

**Source:** *Advanced Player's Guide* (PZO1115), PDF p. 207 / printed p. 206.

**Priority:** A emergency tool. Use only when the rescued roll outweighs two rounds of exposure or when the caster can withdraw from d20-dependent activity.

### 7. Time and Dual-Cursed oracle options are strong but access-dependent

The Time mystery offers Temporal Celerity (roll initiative twice and later always act in surprise rounds), Rewind Time (limited immediate-action rerolls), and Time Sight (true seeing that improves at high levels). The Dual-Cursed archetype adds a second curse, replaces mystery benefits, and supplies Misfortune and Fortune rerolls.

These are not free selections. Demidius's current mystery, curses, archetype, and retraining permissions are not recorded. A ring of revelation also requires the wearer to possess the matching mystery, so it does not bypass the mystery restriction.

**Sources:** *Ultimate Magic* (PZO1117), PDF pp. 57–59 / printed pp. 56–58; *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF p. 174 / printed p. 173.

### 8. Official retraining rules support the current retraining agenda

*Ultimate Campaign* provides procedures for retraining feats, class features, racial traits, skill ranks, and spontaneous spells known. Feat retraining takes 5 days and cannot remove an active prerequisite. A spontaneous spell known takes 2 days per new spell level (1 day for a cantrip/orison), requires a same-type caster who knows the replacement, and must respect the class spell list and spell level.

**Source:** *Ultimate Campaign* (PZO1125), PDF pp. 190–192 / printed pp. 189–191.

**Action:** use these as the default planning costs for Deceitful, Conceal Spell, Divine Fighting Technique, and spell-known changes only if the GM confirms that the campaign follows this subsystem.

### 9. Page of spell knowledge offers permanent spontaneous access

A page of spell knowledge lets a spontaneous caster cast its contained spell with ordinary slots when the spell is on the caster's class list. Like the mnemonic vestment, its interaction with CR-09 depends on whether wizard spells are treated as part of Demidius's usable class list. The page is permanent and slotless but costs the square of spell level times 1,000 gp.

**Source:** *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF p. 315 / printed p. 314.

### 10. Background skills could free Demidius's Profession investment

The Background Skills variant grants two background ranks per character level and classifies Profession as a background skill. Its conversion rule refunds ordinary ranks already spent on background skills while preserving the background investment. At level 17, adoption would create 34 background ranks, subject to normal rank limits and the system's conversion procedure.

**Source:** *Pathfinder Unchained* (PZO1131), PDF pp. 47–50 / printed pp. 46–49.

**Boundary:** this is a campaign-wide optional system, not a personal retraining trick.

### 11. Signature Skill (Profession) can roll sailing checks twice

The Skill Unlocks system allows a character with Signature Skill to unlock one chosen skill. Profession's 10-rank unlock permits rolling twice and taking the better result on Profession checks and always taking 10 to answer professional questions. If applied to Profession (sailor), this is one of the strongest official reliability upgrades for Demidius's ship-driving and sailing checks.

**Source:** *Pathfinder Unchained* (PZO1131), PDF pp. 83 and 87 / printed pp. 82 and 86.

**Priority:** S conditional on Skill Unlocks being adopted and the campaign ship rules treating the relevant roll as a Profession check.

### 12. Ultimate Combat supplies the official siege-crew package

Siege Engineer grants proficiency with all siege weapons and prevents ordinary natural-1 mishaps for non-firearm siege engines. Siege Commander improves assembly and movement work. Master Siege Engineer changes crew loading and aiming from full-round actions to move actions. A diminished crew doubles aiming time, and magical siege engines can receive enhancement bonuses.

**Source:** *Ultimate Combat* (PZO1118), PDF pp. 110, 119, and 159–161 / printed pp. 109, 118, and 158–160.

**Action:** audit Binky's current feats and class-granted proficiencies before adding anything. Master Siege Engineer is the strongest throughput upgrade if she meets its prerequisites.

### 13. Cannon, ammunition, and boarding rules can enrich Dawnrunner combat

*Ultimate Combat* provides cannon construction and misfire rules, blast shot and alchemical ammunition, vehicle-mounted siege procedures, and the corvus boarding bridge. A corvus can attach moving vessels after an opposed driving check, creating a concrete boarding tactic for the Dawnrunner's marines.

**Source:** *Ultimate Combat* (PZO1118), PDF pp. 163–166 / printed pp. 162–165.

**Boundary:** the campaign ship workbooks remain authoritative for vessel statistics and custom weapons.

### 14. Profession (sailor) is an official vehicle-driving skill

The vehicle subsystem uses a driving action every round and lists Profession (sailor) or Knowledge (nature) for many sailing vessels. It provides standard ship examples and weapon capacities, but those examples do not replace the campaign's upgraded galleon statistics.

**Source:** *Ultimate Combat* (PZO1118), PDF pp. 171–172 and 185–187 / printed pp. 170–171 and 184–186.

### 15. Sounon can be modeled with modular downtime infrastructure

*Ultimate Campaign* provides modular rooms, teams, buildings, and organizations. Particularly relevant elements are a Waterfront with docks and shipbuilding facilities, a Magical Repository for spell research, a Hospital, and Sailors teams. Followers can supplement Influence or Labor, and managers reduce the risk of losing control during long absences.

**Source:** *Ultimate Campaign* (PZO1125), PDF pp. 77–114, especially pp. 81, 89–100, and 111–114.

**Priority:** A as an optional planning model for Sounon; do not introduce its capital economy without GM approval.

### 16. Contacts turn followers into an information network

The Contacts subsystem assigns unique NPCs Trust scores and services, while the Leadership discussion treats followers as loyal contacts and encourages using named followers as plot hooks and information sources. This maps naturally onto Demidius's large follower base and named Dawnrunner crew.

**Source:** *Ultimate Campaign* (PZO1125), PDF pp. 147–150.

### 17. Call Beyond the Veil can contact Aristea but cannot free her

This level-6 occult ritual can call a specific deceased spirit or psychic entity from its extraplanar location into the primary caster's body for questioning. It requires difficult Diplomacy, Knowledge (religion), and Knowledge (planes) checks; all casters become exhausted and take a temporary negative level even on success, and failure causes permanent negative levels until removed. The spirit can resist, refuse, or lie.

It may establish communication with Aristea in Tartarus and reveal prison conditions, captors, landmarks, or release requirements. It does not retrieve her soul and Eris or Tartarus may obstruct the attempt.

**Source:** *Occult Adventures* (PZO1132), PDF pp. 211–212 / printed pp. 210–211.

### 18. Robe of arcane heritage is high-value for Okeanikos and Tulip

The robe treats a sorcerer's level as 4 higher for determining usable bloodline powers and their effects. It is therefore a strong candidate for Okeanikos's draconic bloodline/Dragon Disciple progression and Tulip's unicorn bloodline, subject to the exact stacking of Dragon Disciple and their current body-slot items.

**Source:** *Ultimate Equipment* (PZO1123), PDF p. 217 / printed p. 216.

### 19. Roy has two excellent official support spells

*Brilliant inspiration* lets one living creature roll twice and take the better result on attacks, ability checks, and skill checks for its duration, ending early on a natural 20. *Saving finale* is a 1st-level immediate-action bard spell that ends an active bardic performance to let an affected creature reroll a failed save.

**Source:** *Advanced Player's Guide* (PZO1115), PDF pp. 208 and 242 / printed pp. 207 and 241.

**Priority:** Saving Finale A+; Brilliant Inspiration A for encounters with pivotal attack and skill rolls.

### 20. Undine options deserve a racial-trait audit

The Advanced Race Guide gives undines alternate traits and feats including Amphibious, Hydrated Vitality, Nereid Fascination, Aquatic Ancestry, and Hydraulic Maneuver. Aquatic Ancestry grants amphibious capability and improves swim speed, while the Amphibious alternate trait trades away the normal spell-like ability. Maarin and Amparo's existing racial selections must be checked before recommending a replacement.

**Source:** *Advanced Race Guide* (PZO1121), PDF pp. 176–179 / printed pp. 175–178.

## Important limits and negative findings

- The corpus's *Mythic Adventures* volume was already fully indexed in an earlier project pass; this review cross-referenced it but did not duplicate the existing mythic option database.
- Hero Points overlap heavily with the campaign's mythic and probability resources. They should not be added without a campaign-wide balance decision.
- The Ring of Revelation does not provide cross-mystery access; the oracle must possess the matching mystery.
- The Harrow Deck is thematically suitable for probability stories, but its subsystem does not outperform Demidius's existing probability engine.
- Bestiary and NPC Codex entries are encounter/reference material, not automatically available player options.
- Standard vehicle and kingdom statistics do not overwrite the Dawnrunner, Matcha Frappuccino, Sounon, or campaign political rules.
