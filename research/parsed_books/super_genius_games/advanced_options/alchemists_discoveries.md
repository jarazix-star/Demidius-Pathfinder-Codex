# Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries

**Publisher:** Super Genius Games  
**Status:** Complete content-level extraction  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Alchemists' Discoveries*, PDF pp. 2–8.

## Executive Summary

The direct bomb and mutagen options are mostly secondary, but spagyric devices are a major campaign-infrastructure discovery. The system effectively grants a daily reconfigurable pool of temporary magic equipment.

## High-Value Findings

### Spagyric Devices — S-

A level-scaled pool of reconfigurable magic-item-like devices is extremely strong for a ship quartermaster or crafter.

### Mass Production — S-

Duplicate devices become progressively cheaper against the pool, allowing allies and followers to be equipped efficiently.

### Esoteric Studies — A+

Adds broad spell prerequisites from any list, dramatically expanding the device catalogue.

### Homunculus — A-

A replaceable homunculus familiar is valuable for workshops, scouting, and security.

### Explosive Trap — B+

Useful for shipboard or demiplane defense.
