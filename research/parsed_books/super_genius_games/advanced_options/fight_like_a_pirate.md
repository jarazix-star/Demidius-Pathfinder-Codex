# Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate

**Publisher:** Super Genius Games  
**Status:** Complete content-level extraction  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Fight Like a Pirate*, PDF pp. 1–9.

## Executive Summary

The book is far more valuable for the Dawnrunner organization than for Demidius personally. Its strongest material is ship-wide leadership, voyage logistics, and specialist crew archetypes.

## High-Value Findings

### Navigator — S

A bonded navigator can cut travel time or encounter risk, improve crew initiative and awareness, and reroll ship-related rolls.

### Ship's Officer — A

Ship-wide banner and morale support make this an excellent senior-officer chassis.

### Crown Privateer — A

Paladin auras expand to the entire ship, creating an unusually efficient defensive command role.

### Lookout — A-

Long-range perception and illusion resistance fit the Dawnrunner's lookout function extremely well.
