# Advanced Options: Oracle's Curses

**Publisher:** Super Genius Games  
**Status:** Complete content-level extraction  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Oracle's Curses*, PDF pp. 2–5.

## Executive Summary

This book contains several mechanically aggressive curses, but none is an obvious safe retrain for Demidius. It is most valuable as a source of curse design precedents and high-risk optimization.

## High-Value Findings

### Addict — A*

Equalizing lower-level spell DCs to the highest castable spell level is exceptionally powerful, but the drug dependency is severe.

### Drunkard — A*

Can generate an enormous Charisma-check bonus, but creates addiction and Wisdom-check liabilities.

### Provocative — A-

A major Diplomacy increase fits the Influence pillar, though it worsens Demidius's existing social blind spot.

### Second Curse — B*

Potentially unlocks combinations, but every pairing must be evaluated for permanent liabilities.

### Star-Crossed — D

Directly undermines the Probability Engine by converting natural 11s into automatic failures.

13 oracle curses and 4 curse feats.
