# Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes

**Publisher:** Super Genius Games  
**Status:** Complete content-level extraction  
**Source:** *Advanced Options: Witches' Hexes*, PDF pp. 2–6.

## Executive Summary

This is the strongest Demidius-facing book in the batch because Spirit Guide and custom divine systems can make hexes relevant. Several options are action denial, save debuffing, preparation, leadership, and strategic flexibility.

## High-Value Findings

### Doom Gaze — S-

A penalty that can reach –8 to saving throws is an extraordinary enchantment setup if Demidius can legally acquire it.

### Mock — A+

Staggering a target for up to level rounds attacks enemy action economy directly.

### Rune Hex — A+

Turns hexes into prepared trigger effects, supporting security, contingencies, and infrastructure.

### Wish Giver — S

A free limited wish for another creature once/day is campaign-scale flexibility.

### Whispers — A

Half level to a skill can enhance Diplomacy, Spellcraft, crafting, or Profession (sailor).

### Retainers — A+

A major organizational ability that exchanges a cohort for numerous special retainers.

## Extraction Totals

16 hexes, 8 major hexes, 5 grand hexes, and 4 feats.
