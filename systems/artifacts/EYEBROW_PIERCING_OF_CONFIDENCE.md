# Eyebrow Piercing of Confidence

Demidius's legendary item grants +4 luck to Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma, increased to +6 by Fortune's Child and Fate's Favored. It also increases the DC of Fatal Flaws by 5.

The ability-score increase is not itself +6 directly to every derived roll; recalculate the corresponding ability modifiers from the adjusted scores.
