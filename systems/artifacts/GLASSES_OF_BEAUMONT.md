# Glasses of Beaumont

Gifted by Queen Lidda Beaumont of Nysia after she slew Ares during the Culling.

- **3/day:** reveal a creature's alignment unerringly.
- **3/day:** use *true seeing*.

The glasses provide information superiority in diplomacy, investigation, divine politics, and encounters involving illusions, invisibility, shapechanging, or magical disguise.
