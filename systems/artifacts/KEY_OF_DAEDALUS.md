# Key of Daedalus

A brass key overflowing with magic.

- Full-round action to unlock any mundane or magical lock.
- Godly magical locks require a spell-penetration check.
- Creates an extradimensional corridor 3/day to a previously visited plane and location.
- Corridor remains one week and may be used by anyone or anything.
- Wielder is immune to *maze*.
- Within the Labyrinth, any opened door may be declared the exit.
- First use permanently grants the Paranoia Fatal Flaw.
