# Inherited Demiplane

## Time control

Time may run three times faster or three times slower than outside time.

## Dionysian nexus

The demiplane appears partly built around a bottle of Dionysus's full-strength wine. It produces one bottle of watered-down Dionysian wine each month.

## Gate

- Stone circle.
- Radius: 10 feet.
- Requires the key to open.

## Sleep blessing

A creature sleeping in the demiplane:

- becomes fully healed;
- permanently gains +50 maximum hit points the first time only;
- permanently gains the Loyalty Fatal Flaw, triggered only in relation to the owner.

## Confirmed recipients

| Character | Full healing | +50 max HP | Loyalty target |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Demidius | Yes | Yes | Demidius |
| Aristea | Yes | Yes | Demidius |
