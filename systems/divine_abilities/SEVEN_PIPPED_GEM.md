# Seven-Pipped Gem

![The Seven-Pipped Gem branching into divine interventions](../../docs/assets/engines/divine-abilities.png)

Seven-Pipped Gem is a divine ability, not an artifact.

At 17 HD it grants +8 luck to any d20 roll, or +17 for Gambling and Sleight of Hand. Fortune's Child and Fate's Favored each add +1, producing +10 or +19. It may be declared after seeing the die but before the GM announces success or failure, uses an immediate action, and is usable up to Demidius's Charisma-bonus uses.
